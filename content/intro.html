Infection Alert

Infections raise your resting heart rate, sometimes even before you can feel the symptoms.

This app analyzes heart rate from the Apple Watch shows you if your heart rate is elevated above your personal baseline.

Anonamiyzed heart rates and approximate location are stored to a public server. So we can study the spread of the infection.

Any researcher will be able to access the data.

This is not a coronavirus detection app. Any type infection or other things like being overworked or tranining too hard could elevate your heart rate durring sleep.

Open Source and Open Data

The source code of this app is available on github at https://github.com/unrelatedlabs/infection-alert.

Access to the public data is available at https://github.com/unrelatedlabs/infection-alert/tree/master/data.
